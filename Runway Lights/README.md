***Runway Lights***


KEY[0] is a reset. SW[1:0] as inputs indicate the wind direction: Calm (SW[1:0] = 00), 
Right to Left (SW[1:0] = 01), Left to Right (SW[1:0] = 10). 
Besides, LEDR[2:0] as outputs show the corresponding sequence of lights.
